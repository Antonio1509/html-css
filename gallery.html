Explore every aspect of art.


Bai Tu Long Bay, Vietnam

Landscape

This is the lesser-known equivalent of the more crowded Halong Bay, offering the same staggering land and seascapes with dramatic eruptions of rock scattered throughout the bay, best explored on an overnight cruise so you can enjoy the beautiful karst scenery at sunset and sunrise.


Mount Fuji, Japan

Landscape

Surrounded by forests that blaze with color in autumn, this area is one of Japans most breathtaking landscapes. The sight of Mount Fuji reflected in the serene waters of the lake is nothing short of unforgettable.


Punakha Dzong, Bhutan

landscape

Cut through by rushing rivers and surrounded by the mighty Himalayan backdrop, the scenery around Punakha is magnificent. Marvel at the serene situation of the Punakha Dzong at the confluence of the Pho Chhu and Mo Chhu rivers, or admire views of the whole valley from the lofty temple of Khamsum Yulley Namgyal.


Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

Landscape

Mount Kilimanjaro is the highest peak in all of Africa, standing at a sky-piercing height of 5,895 meters. It rises from the surrounding plains in isolation, making it one of the most impressive sights on the continent. Whether you view from afar or brave the summit, this is undeniably one of Africas most iconic landscapes.


The Mona Lisa

Portrait

The Mona Lisa is one of the most famous portraits in art history, both the technical aspect and the subject have raised a lot of interest over the centuries.


Vincent Van Gogh

Portrait

Vincent Van Gogh painted at least 35 self-portraits, he used himself to practice painting people, and many of them become the famous portraits we know today.


The painting Lady with Fan

Portrait

The painting Lady with Fan by the famous artist Gustav Klimt is an extraordinary work of art. It stands out among his other works as one of the most remarkable and breathtaking.


Girl with the Pearl

Portrait

Johannes Vermeers iconic painting, Girl with the Pearl, is a captivating work of art. Created in 1665, the painting depicts an unnamed young woman wearing a bright blue and yellow turban and a lovely pearl earring.


Parthenon, Athens, Greece.

Architecture

A masterpiece of Ancient Greek architecture featuring massive Doric columns and perfect symmetry. It served as a temple dedicated to the goddess Athena and remains a global symbol of classical beauty.


Sydney Opera House, Sydney

Architecture

Famous for its unique "sail-like" roof structure, this building is a landmark of 20th-century Expressionist design. It sits on the edge of the harbor, blending modern engineering with an organic, shell-like aesthetic.


Fallingwater, Mill Run, Pennsylvania

Architecture

Designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, this home is built directly over a waterfall to blend man-made structures with nature. It is the ultimate example of "Organic Architecture," where the building feels like a natural part of the landscape.


Burj Khalifa, Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Architecture

Holding the record as the world's tallest building, this skyscraper is a marvel of modern high-rise engineering. Its sleek, silver exterior and needle-like spire represent the pinnacle of futuristic urban architecture.
